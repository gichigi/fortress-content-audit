allowed-tools: Read

Rate this implementation across these dimensions:

1. **Simplicity**: Is it as simple but effective as it could be? (1-10)
2. **Correctness**: Does it solve the problem correctly? (1-10)
3. **Style**: Is there anything to clean up or improve? (1-10)
4. **Efficiency**: Could it be more efficient? (1-10)
5. **Maintainability**: Will future developers understand it? (1-10)

For each dimension, explain:
- Why you gave that score
- What's working well
- What could be improved

Provide an overall rating and 1-2 sentence summary.
